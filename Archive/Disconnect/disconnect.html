Disconnect

Once in a while, I uninstall my WhatsApp, along with social media apps like Reddit and Instagram. These things always get me overwhelmed.

Yesterday I uninstalled my WhatsApp for the nth time, I am on break from Instagram and Reddit already. I turned on grey-scale mode.

That's the standard these days, for minimising the distraction from phone.

I was reading a blog of Manu (I am lazy enough to link the exact article) where he mentioned that minimalism is a tool and not an end.

When we define a purpose, or a goal, which we want to achive from something, declutter becomes easy and there is no sentiment afterwards.

I remember 10yrs back learning was different, we used to learn from books/pdfs. We had to ask our friends for the pdf and get it on the pendrive. There was no internet back then. It actually used to give a lot of joy just to get connected to internet for 10 minutes.

But can I really get offline ? I have my online classes, then if I want to learn a new skill, or see some design inspiration I definitely have to look up on internet. (Is there any way? Do tell me. )

Hmm, let's see how my "Disconnection" goes around this time!

P.S: I have started to dislike wired earphones these days, and I prefer music on speaker. It is much more fun.
