impuse

We all have a history of taking decisions based on impulse. Whether it be purchase of keyboard to buying Minecraft and never playing with it anyway, all these decisions sometimes become a bitter memory.

To avoid impulsive purchase the use of trial version of the product is important since it would give an overall idea of what is going inside the system rather than looking at it from the “Consumerism” perspective.

The trial period can vary from 10 days to 30 days and no more than that. If we use the product on a regular scale, it is worth purchasing. However, if you give up in the meantime, it a waste of money.  

This slow approach can help in minimizing the digital consumption as well as prevent us from the aftermath of regret.
